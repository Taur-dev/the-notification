Depth parallax — the eye

Move your mouse across the image · tilt your phone · or just let it drift. The reflected man & car are lifted onto a nearer plane than the iris, so the little figure floats inside the eye as your viewpoint moves.

Note: this uses an approximate depth map built with classical computer vision (the reflection lifted onto a near plane via convex hull). A neural depth pass or a hand-painted map would separate the fur, lashes, and reflection into cleaner layers. This is the feel test — strength, drift, and tilt are all tunable.
