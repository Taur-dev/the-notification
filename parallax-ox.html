Depth parallax — prototype

Move your mouse across the image · tilt your phone · or just let it drift. The ox sits on a nearer plane than the sky, so the perspective shifts as your viewpoint moves.

Note: this uses an approximate depth map built with classical computer vision (the ox segmented as the near plane, blended with a ground gradient). A neural depth pass or a hand-painted map would sharpen the man & car into their own midground layer. This is the feel test — strength, drift, and tilt are all tunable.
